ABOUT US

Project Idea


Being surrounded by music almost all the time we noticed that all the platforms out there, even if they do offer playlists based on our taste it lacks character and depth. We wanted to have a playlist that actually resonated with our taste and something that was not only based upon a learning model but took in account our actual feelings and moods. Hence we cam up with Soul Train, this is our shot at making sure that the playlist we curate for you reflect the type of music you want and the type that would go with your mood. Enjoy.

How It Works


Mood Gradient is made possible by a random forest ensemble of 10 neural networks that were trained on popular community mood-based playlists, and rated almost 2 million new songs into these categories with great accuracy. The data was acquired via Spotify API calls and the models are TensorFlow sequential networks. Since the models were individually trained on different pieces of the data, they can tell you not only which mood a song conveys, but also to what degree (for example, 64% happy, 14% chill) which was pivotal in arranging seamlessÂ gradients.
